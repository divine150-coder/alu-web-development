I am much afraid that schools will prove to be the great gates of hell unless they diligently labor in explaining the Holy Scriptures and engraving them in the hearts of the youth. I advise no one to place his child where the Scriptures do not reign paramount. Every institution where men are not unceasingly occupied with the Word of God must become corrupt.

Guidelines

The Bible is a remarkable fountain: the more one draws and drinks of it, the more it stimulates thirst. One can never come away from it sated. It is an inexhaustible well. Therefore, it is impossible for a Christian to be sufficiently armed against the spirit of lies and error except with the divine weapon of the Word of God.

Content

Be a sinner, and let your sins be strong (sin boldly), but let your trust in Christ be stronger, and rejoice in Christ who is the victor over sin, death, and the world. We will commit sins while we are here, for this life is not a place where justice resides. We, however, says Peter (2. Peter 3:13) are looking forward to a new heaven and a new earth where justice will reign

I have so much to do that I shall spend the first three hours in prayer. It is not necessary, indeed it is not even possible, to work and to toil, and to labor from early until late without intermission. To be always full of cares and always worried; to study and strive and labor, but with such a disposition of mind that no time is left for prayer or for God; this is not good.
